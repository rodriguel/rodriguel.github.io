Webpage of R.L.

I am a postdoc under the supervision of Virginie Ehrlacher, Tony Lelièvre and Julien Reygner at Ecole des Ponts (CERMICS), affiliated to the Matherials team (INRIA). I am endorsed by the HighLEAP ERC grant. Previously, I was a PhD student under the supervision of Mathieu Lewin at Université Paris-Dauphine. My research interests mainly revolves around analysis and mathematical physics, including questions related to optimal transport and statistical mechanics.
